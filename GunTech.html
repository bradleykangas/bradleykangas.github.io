Gun Tech (2022):

Gun Tech is a VR shooting gallery created for CSC 475 - Topics in Computer Science.
The game was designed to be picked up and played by many people at a convention,
so focus on delivering an experience that was brief and intuitive was the goal. Players
start in an empty room then are given instruction as a single gun falls from above.

We had a two-month development window and I developed the lock-on system for
shooting multiple targets at once, as well as the player controls.

The game is designed to be played on Oculus / Meta Quest and was played by
antendees at UNCW WITX Conference 2022.

Development Role:

Game Design, Programming, 3D Assets, UI, Team Management, Editing

Tools:

Unity C#, Autodesk Maya, Visual Studio, Paint.NET, Sony VEGAS
